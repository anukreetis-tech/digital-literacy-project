### Task 1: Digital Literacy Infographic

This infographic was created using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. 

The design covers key areas such as the definition of digital literacy, useful digital tools for students, safe internet practices, professional online presence, 
and email etiquette. Simple text and visuals were used to make the information clear, engaging, and easy to understand.

The goal of this infographic is to raise awareness among students about using digital technologies responsibly, safely, and effectively in their academic and personal 
lives.
