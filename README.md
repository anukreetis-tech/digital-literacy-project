## Project Overview
This project focuses on developing essential digital literacy skills required for academic and professional growth. It includes practical tasks such as creating an infographic, building a digital portfolio, exploring online platforms, practicing email etiquette, and understanding cybercrime awareness.

The aim of this project is to promote responsible, safe, and effective use of digital tools and technologies.

---

## Task Summaries

### Task 1: Infographic  
In this task, I designed a digital infographic using Canva to explain the concept of digital literacy. The infographic highlights key areas such as digital tools, online safety, professional online presence, and email etiquette. The aim was to present information in a visually appealing and easy-to-understand format, helping improve both creativity and communication skills.

### Task 2: Digital Portfolio  
This task involved creating and updating my profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle, and Stack Overflow. Each platform was used to build a professional digital identity and showcase skills. This activity helped me understand the importance of maintaining an online presence for future academic and career opportunities.

### Task 3: Platforms  
In this task, I explored coding and collaboration platforms by solving a beginner-level problem and creating a Google Form quiz on digital literacy. It helped me improve my problem-solving skills and understand how online tools can be used for learning, collaboration, and data collection.

### Task 4: Email Etiquette  
This task focused on professional communication. I wrote two formal emails—one for requesting an assignment extension and another for applying for an internship. Additionally, I created a checklist for responsible social media use, which improved my understanding of proper online behavior.

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness  
In this task, I studied cybercrime with a focus on UPI and online payment fraud. I prepared a case study based on a real incident and created a prevention checklist. This task increased my awareness of online threats and the importance of following safe digital practices.

---

## Learning Outcome
Through this project, I learned the importance of digital responsibility, online safety, professional communication, and building a strong digital presence. It also improved my understanding of real-world applications of technology.
