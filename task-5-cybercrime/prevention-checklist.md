## Stay Safe Online: UPI & Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

### ✔ Do's
- Always verify payment requests before approving them.  
- Use strong passwords and enable app locks for banking apps.  
- Check the authenticity of links before clicking.  
- Keep your mobile number and bank details updated securely.  
- Immediately report suspicious activity to your bank.  

### ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share your UPI PIN, OTP, or bank details with anyone.  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages.  
- Do not install unknown apps from untrusted sources.  
- Do not ignore small unauthorized transactions.  

### 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips
- Always remember: money is received without entering a UPI PIN.  
- Never approve “collect requests” unless you trust the sender.  
- Use official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm only.  
- Regularly check your bank transaction history.  

### 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
