### Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

|                           Do's ✅                            |                         Don'ts ❌                         |
|                                                              |                                                            |
| Think before posting and ensure content is appropriate.      | Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.   |
| Maintain respectful and polite communication.                | Avoid using offensive or harmful language.                 |
| Use privacy settings to protect your profile.                | Do not accept requests from unknown people blindly.        |
| Share meaningful and useful content.                         | Avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.               |
| Build a positive and professional online presence.           | Do not post content that can harm your reputation.         |
